About Atax Company Ltd

Atax Company Ltd is a leading provider of comprehensive networking and security solutions across Kenya. Established with a vision to deliver reliable, high-speed connectivity and cutting-edge surveillance, we cater to both residential and commercial clients.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our robust fiber network installations, advanced CCTV systems, and meticulous TV mounting services. We pride ourselves on our expert team, swift response times, and a client-centric approach that ensures tailored solutions for every unique requirement.
